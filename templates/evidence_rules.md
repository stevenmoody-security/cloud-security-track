# Evidence Rules

✅ OK:
- Redacted screenshots of settings and findings
- Short log snippets (non-sensitive)
- Compliance/finding summaries

❌ Never:
- Access keys, secret keys, tokens, passwords
- Private keys (.pem/.key)
- Terraform state files
- Anything you wouldn’t want indexed publicly

Redaction:
- If unsure, redact or omit.
